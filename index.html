About Me

I am graduated in Electrical Engineering (Communication) at University of Tehran, Iran. I am affiliated with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering , and I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Behnam Bahrak.

My primary area of research is graph theory and Algorithms and phisical layer security. I am also interested in machine learning. My interests lie in the intersection of computer science, applied mathematics and telecommunication engineering. Generally, I am interested in achieving better theoretical understanding of graph theory and machine learning, as well as designing practical and provable algorithms for graph theory and machine learning problems.
