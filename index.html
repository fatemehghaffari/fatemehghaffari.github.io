About Me

I am graduated in Electrical Engineering (Communications) at the University of Tehran, Iran. I am affiliated with the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering , and I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Behnam Bahrak.

My primary area of research is graph theory and Algorithms and physical layer security. I am also interested in coding theory and machine learning. Generally, My interests lie in the intersection of computer science, applied mathematics, and telecommunication engineering. Generally, I am interested in achieving a better theoretical understanding of graph theory and coding theory, as well as designing practical and provable algorithms for graph theory and machine learning problems.
